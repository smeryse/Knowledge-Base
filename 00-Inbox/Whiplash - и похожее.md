If you enjoyed "Whiplash," a highly intense and gripping drama about a talented jazz drummer and his obsessive music instructor, here are some movie recommendations that you may also like:

"Black Swan" (2010) - A psychological thriller about a ballerina who strives for perfection and the mental breakdown she experiences during the process.

"Birdman" (2014) - A dark comedy-drama about a former superhero actor who tries to revive his career by directing and starring in a Broadway play.

"The Social Network" (2010) - A biographical drama about the founding of Facebook and the ruthless ambition and legal battles between its co-founders.

"Nightcrawler" (2014) - A thriller about a freelance crime journalist who becomes increasingly obsessed with capturing the most shocking footage, regardless of the ethical and legal implications.

"There Will Be Blood" (2007) - A drama about a ruthless oil prospector in the early 20th century who will stop at nothing to achieve his goals, even if it means destroying those around him.

I hope you find these recommendations interesting and enjoyable!
